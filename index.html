Building the Future, Together

Established on 16 May 2023 in JAFZA by Mr. Roin Baratashvili and Mrs. Lela Bolkvadze, RWN Construction FZCO specializes in trading top-quality building and construction materials. Our aim is to lead the market in the Middle East, building a reputation for excellence.

Our Foundation

Leveraging a solid base of experience in nuclear construction, luxury real estate, logistics, and international transport, our founders drive RWN towards success, ensuring expert knowledge and industry insight.

Our Commitment

We emphasize quality products, reliable service, and strong partnerships, establishing RWN FZCO as a trusted provider for contractors, developers, and construction professionals across the industry.

Our Mission

"Our mission is to provide a comprehensive range of top-quality building materials, exceptional service, and value-added solutions to our customers in the construction industry in Dubai. Through our expertise, reliability, and commitment to excellence, we aim to be the preferred supplier and trusted partner, facilitating the successful completion of construction projects and contributing to the growth and development of UAE region environment."
